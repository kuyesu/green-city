Our incredible approach to clean city

  • Collecting

    Imagine walking through the streets of a city and seeing trash scattered everywhere. It's an eyesore, and it can be harmful to the environment. But what if there was a solution? A smart dustbin and mobile app that rewards users for collecting and disposing of waste properly.

    Collect waste

  • Reward Users

    By using the Smart City system, users are not only helping the environment, but they are also contributing to the local economy. It's a win-win situation for everyone involved, and it's all made possible through the power of technology.

    Rewards

  • Sort the wastes

    But Smart City doesn't stop at waste collection. The system also includes a waste sorting that ensures waste is properly sorted and recycled. This means that even the tiniest item of waste can be repurposed and reused, making the city cleaner and more sustainable.

    Sorting

  • Recycling

    Once the waste is sorted, it's time to recycle. Smart City works with local recycling companies to turn the waste into useful products such as chairs, manure, and other materials. These products are then sold back to the community, creating a sustainable cycle of waste disposal and product creation.
